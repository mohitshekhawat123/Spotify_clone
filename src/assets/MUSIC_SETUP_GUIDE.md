# Music Files Setup Guide

## Required Music Files

To complete the Spotify clone, you need to add the following MP3 files to this `assets` folder:

1. `happy_nations.mp3` - For "Happy Nations" song
2. `gal_sun.mp3` - For "Gal Sun" song
3. `midnight_dreams.mp3` - For "Midnight Dreams" song
4. `ocean_waves.mp3` - For "Ocean Waves" song
5. `electric_pulse.mp3` - For "Electric Pulse" song
6. `mountain_high.mp3` - For "Mountain High" song
7. `sunset_boulevard.mp3` - For "Sunset Boulevard" song
8. `purple_rain.mp3` - For "Purple Rain" song
9. `golden_hour.mp3` - For "Golden Hour" song
10. `neon_lights.mp3` - For "Neon Lights" song

## Where to Get Free Music

### Free Royalty-Free Music Sources:

1. **Free Music Archive (FMA)**
   - Website: https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - License: Various (check individual tracks)
   - Great for diverse genres

2. **Incompetech (Kevin MacLeod)**
   - Website: https://incompetech.com/music/royalty-free/
   - License: CC BY 3.0
   - Wide variety of instrumental music

3. **Bensound**
   - Website: https://www.bensound.com/
   - License: Free with attribution
   - Professional quality tracks

4. **YouTube Audio Library**
   - Website: https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary
   - License: Free to use
   - Large collection of music

5. **Pixabay Music**
   - Website: https://pixabay.com/music/
   - License: Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required

6. **Freesound**
   - Website: https://freesound.org/
   - License: Various CC licenses
   - Community-driven

7. **Jamendo**
   - Website: https://www.jamendo.com/
   - License: Various licenses available
   - Independent artists

## How to Add Music Files

1. Download MP3 files from any of the sources above
2. Rename them to match the required filenames listed above
3. Place them in the `spotify-clone/src/assets/` folder
4. The app will automatically pick them up

## Note on Copyright

- Always check the license of any music you download
- Ensure you have proper rights to use the music
- For commercial projects, consider purchasing licenses
- Some tracks may require attribution
